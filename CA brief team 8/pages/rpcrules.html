"Game Rule"

A traditional two-player game. Each round begins with the players saying, "rock, paper, scissors, shoot!" Each participant holds out their fist for a rock, their flat hand for paper, or their middle and index fingers for scissors when the game is called "shoot." Paper covers rock, rock crushes scissors, and scissors cut paper. Check out who prevails in each round! In other games, this is an excellent way to determine who goes first
